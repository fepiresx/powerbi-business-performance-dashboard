# 📊 Business Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze business performance based on sales, profit, and segmentation across different countries.

The goal is to transform raw data into actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Excel (Data Source)
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Performance by Product

* Sales distribution by product
* Average sale price analysis
* Sales trends by segment over time

### 2. Sales and Profit Analysis by Country

* Total sales and units sold
* Profit distribution across countries
* Profit trends over time
* Sales comparison by country

### 3. Business Performance by Country and Segment

* Sales and units sold by country (map)
* Profit by country (map)
* Profit distribution by segment
* Key business insights

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* The United States leads in total sales but does not have the highest profit margin.
* Government segment dominates profit contribution across regions.
* There is a clear gap between sales volume and profitability across countries.
